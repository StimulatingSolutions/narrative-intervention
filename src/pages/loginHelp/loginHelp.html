Login Help Account Setup When your account is first set up, you will receive an email with the subject "Welcome to the NS app, [your name]". That email contains a link that will let you set your password. If you can't find this email, please check your Spam folder. Forgotten Password If you have forgotten your password, enter your email address as normal for logging in, and then use the Change Password button. This will send you an email with the subject "Your Narrative Structure password has been reset", and containing a link that will let you change your password. If you can't find this email, please check your Spam folder. Forgotten Email Address If you can't remember which email address is associated with your Narrative Structure app account, please contact a Narrative Structure administrator for help. How-To Videos If you are a Researcher or Admin, once you log in you can use the How-To Videos button to watch relevant videos on how to set up and use the Narrative Structure app. Other Help For any other help, please contact a Narrative Structure administrator.
